About Us

A Luxurious Hotel
with Allahabadi Tradition

Hira Inn, a centrally air conditioned hotel is located in business friendly commercial area Kareli , Allahabad, at a distance of just 3 km from Allahabad railway station and 8 km from airport. Being fully equipped with all kinds of modern amenities and services delivered by professionally trained staff brings an impression of staying at home away from home. Because of being centrally located ,it connects to all nearby popular tourist places and local markets easily within a blink of eye. Major attraction of Allahabad includes Anand Bhawan Museum, Chandrsekhar Azad Park, The Allahabad Museum, Triveni Sangam.
